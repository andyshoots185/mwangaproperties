We're Changing the Real Estate Landscape in Uganda

Mwanga Properties is a premier real estate company based in Kampala, Uganda, offering comprehensive property solutions including sales, rentals, construction, and interior design services.

Founded in 2010, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the Ugandan real estate market, with a portfolio of luxury properties and successful projects across the country.

Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to delivering exceptional service and creating spaces that inspire and enrich lives.
